## Notes

- PNG files also work - just rename the service extensions if needed (or rename files to `.jpg`)
- Missing files silently fall back to gradient/initials - no broken image icons
- Recommended size: movies ~800x450px (landscape), characters ~400x400px (square or portrait), stones ~80x80px (hexagon)
